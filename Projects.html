Mar.io's Apocalypse


The Mar.io Level
Mar.io in Unity
Enemy Death Animation
❮ ❯

Explore a grassy wasteland as the robot Mar.io, destroying robotic GOOMBAS with lasers. This project was created in Unity2D as a reskin of classic Nintendo game Mario. It was created as a way to practice sprite editing, using Unity's Animator, and just to refamiliarize myself with Unity and how it works since I haven't used it in a long time. It will be hosted on itch.io soon. Click on the images or title to play the game!
