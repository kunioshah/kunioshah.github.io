I designed the water polo page as I had experience in the sport and with the players from my years on the team. I captured and edited photos, conducted interviews, wrote the captions and body copy with interviews from players, and designed the layout of the page. Both photo packages were cut circularly, to resemble balls floating in the water; for the mod, I interviewed players on “Who do you steal sunscreen from?”, a common undertaking when players forget their own.

I also designed an interrupter page. For this page, I had to come up with a concept for a page from scratch. Since this page came before our class themes for the year, which were Cartoon Shows (ie: Spongebob), I themed the page around childhood cartoon shows. I asked interviewees to pose like their favorite cartoon character and photographed them; I cut out the background, edited the photos, and drew the character’s outfit onto them. I put the images onto a paper background, along with black and white doodles, to give the impression of drawings on a notebook.
